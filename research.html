Constraining Dark Matter Annihilation in dSphs

Advisor: Prof. Louis Strigari  | Texas A&M University

I've recently started a new project under Prof. Strigari, where I will be using the updated 16 years of Fermi-LAT data to do a combined analysis of dSphs. I will also use J-factors calculated for velocity-dependent annihilation to analyze the velocity-dependent annihilation in these dSphs.

Constraining Dark Matter Annihilation in the LMC

Advisor: Prof. Louis Strigari  | Texas A&M University

I analyzed gamma-ray emission from the Large Magellanic Cloud using Fermi-LAT data to place constraints on dark matter annihilation cross sections. This involved spatial modeling, J-factor construction, and spectral fitting.
