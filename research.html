Constraining Dark Matter Annihilation in the LMC

Advisor: Prof. X  |  Methods: Fermi-LAT, Fermipy, likelihood analysis

I analyzed gamma-ray emission from the Large Magellanic Cloud using Fermi-LAT data to place constraints on dark matter annihilation cross sections. This involved spatial modeling, J-factor construction, and spectral fitting.
